## Welcome to Vault

Vault is a comprehensive collection of cross-platform projects spanning security, networking, and system administration domains. Each project in the Vault collection is maintained at different lifecycle stages, from experimental to production-ready tools.

<Note>
  Vault follows a non-standard workflow where published commits may be rewritten or removed without notice as part of the debugging and improvement process.
</Note>

## Project Lifecycle Stages

Each project in Vault is tagged with a lifecycle badge that indicates its current development status. Understanding these stages helps you choose the right tool for your needs.

### Experimental
<img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/status-experimental-orange.svg" alt="Experimental" />

Projects in the experimental stage are actively evolving and may change without notice. These are cutting-edge tools that are still finding their final form.

- **Status**: May change without notice
- **Use Case**: Testing new features and approaches
- **Stability**: Expect breaking changes

### Beta
<img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/status-beta-magenta.svg" alt="Beta" />

Beta projects are undergoing testing with minor adjustments ongoing. The core functionality is established, but refinements continue.

- **Status**: Testing and minor adjustments ongoing
- **Use Case**: Early adoption with feedback
- **Stability**: Core features stable, details may change

### Release Candidate
<img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/status-release_candidate-skyblue.svg" alt="Release Candidate" />

Release candidate projects are nearly production-ready with only critical fixes expected.

- **Status**: Only critical fixes expected
- **Use Case**: Pre-production testing
- **Stability**: Very stable, minimal changes

### Frozen
<img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/status-frozen-blue.svg" alt="Frozen" />

Frozen projects have halted development with no planned changes. They remain available for use in their current state.

- **Status**: Development halted, no planned changes
- **Use Case**: Stable, unchanging tools
- **Stability**: No future modifications

### Deprecated
<img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/status-deprecated-red.svg" alt="Deprecated" />

Deprecated projects are obsolete and will receive no further updates. Consider alternative solutions.

- **Status**: Obsolete project, no further updates
- **Use Case**: Legacy support only
- **Stability**: No maintenance or bug fixes
